Stream Information

Select a stream that aligns with your interests and future career aspirations:

Science Stream

The Science stream focuses on subjects like Mathematics, Physical Science, and Life Sciences. Students who enjoy problem-solving and understanding the natural world often thrive here.

Potential Careers:

  • Doctor
  • Engineer
  • Pharmacist
  • Scientist
  • Environmental Specialist

Commerce Stream

The Commerce stream includes subjects such as Accounting, Economics, and Business Studies. It is ideal for students who are interested in finance, trade, and the business world.

Potential Careers:

  • Accountant
  • Economist
  • Business Manager
  • Financial Analyst
  • Entrepreneur

Humanities (Arts) Stream

The Humanities stream covers subjects such as History, Geography, and Languages. It’s perfect for students who are interested in social sciences, culture, and human behavior.

Potential Careers:

  • Journalist
  • Psychologist
  • Teacher
  • Lawyer
  • Social Worker

Technical Stream

The Technical stream includes practical subjects like Engineering Graphics and Design (EGD), Mechanical Technology, and Electrical Technology. It is suited for students who have a knack for hands-on work and technology.

Potential Careers:

  • Technician
  • Electrician
  • Mechanic
  • Construction Manager
  • Architect

Agricultural Stream

The Agricultural stream focuses on subjects like Agricultural Sciences and Agricultural Management Practices. It is ideal for students interested in farming, sustainability, and managing natural resources.

Potential Careers:

  • Agronomist
  • Farm Manager
  • Veterinarian
  • Environmental Scientist
  • Food Scientist

Hospitality Stream

This stream focuses on Hospitality Studies, Consumer Studies, and related fields. It’s perfect for students who want to work in the food, lodging, or tourism industries.

Potential Careers:

  • Chef
  • Hotel Manager
  • Event Coordinator
  • Restaurant Manager
  • Travel Consultant

Arts & Culture Stream

This stream is designed for students who are passionate about creative subjects such as Drama, Music, Visual Arts, and Dance. It is perfect for those interested in pursuing careers in the creative industry.

Potential Careers:

  • Artist
  • Musician
  • Actor/Actress
  • Fashion Designer
  • Graphic Designer

Information Technology (IT) Stream

The IT stream focuses on Computer Science, Information Technology, and related subjects. It’s ideal for students who are interested in technology, coding, and digital solutions.

Potential Careers:

  • Software Developer
  • Web Developer
  • Network Engineer
  • Data Analyst
  • Cybersecurity Specialist

Health and Social Care Stream

This stream is suited for students interested in health, well-being, and social care services. It focuses on healthcare studies, child development, and community care.

Potential Careers:

  • Nurse
  • Social Worker
  • Occupational Therapist
  • Childcare Worker
  • Healthcare Assistant

Tourism Stream

The Tourism stream prepares students for careers in the tourism and hospitality industry. Subjects include Travel and Tourism Studies, Business Studies, and Hospitality Studies.

Potential Careers:

  • Travel Agent
  • Tour Guide
  • Event Planner
  • Tourism Manager
  • Hotel Manager

Engineering and Technology Stream

This stream focuses on advanced technical subjects such as Electrical Technology, Mechanical Technology, and Civil Technology, preparing students for engineering careers.

Potential Careers:

  • Civil Engineer
  • Mechanical Engineer
  • Electrical Engineer
  • Architect
  • Industrial Engineer

Sports Science Stream

The Sports Science stream is designed for students interested in physical education, fitness, and sports management. It combines biology, physical education, and sports theory.

Potential Careers:

  • Sports Coach
  • Fitness Trainer
  • Sports Physiotherapist
  • Sports Scientist
  • Sports Manager
